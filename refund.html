Refund Policy

Our commitment to fair and transparent refunds.

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

1. General Policy

At Nightfall Services, we stand behind the quality of our products. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help. Please read this policy carefully before requesting a refund.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

  • Digital Products: Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase if the product has not been activated or used.
  • Software Licenses: Refunds are available within 3 days of purchase if the license has not been redeemed or activated.
  • Subscription Services: Refunds are available for the first 3 days of your subscription period.
  • Custom Services: Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us directly.

3. Non-Refundable Items

  • Products that have been activated or used
  • Custom or personalized services
  • Products purchased more than 7 days ago (or 3 days for licenses)
  • Gift cards or store credit
  • Products where the buyer has violated our Terms of Service

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please:

  • Contact us via our Discord server with your order details
  • Include your product name, purchase date, and reason for refund
  • Our team will review your request within 24-48 hours

5. Processing Time

Once your refund is approved, it will be processed within 5-10 business days. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

6. Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds for:

  • Products that did not meet your expectations but have been partially used
  • Services that were partially delivered
  • Technical issues that were resolved but caused inconvenience

7. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you file a chargeback or dispute without first requesting a refund through our official channels, we reserve the right to suspend your account and permanently ban you from our services.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Refund Policy at any time. Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us via our Discord server.
